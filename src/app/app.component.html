Explore the world with a smile

Travelaja is a Latin American international organization dedicated to promoting social and intercultural development year after year. We aim to create new opportunities for young people between the ages of 18 and 25. As part of our mission, we offer an annual lottery of 20,000 visas to various countries around the world, giving applicants the freedom to choose their preferred destination. In 2025, we are launching a new call for applications, inviting young people from across Latin America to participate and apply for a visa to the country of their choice.
